Features

Built for noisy threads, not for drama

ThreadSense is a reading aid. It highlights patterns so you can slow down, open the popover, and decide—never auto-reporting, blocking, or posting on your behalf.

◆

Per-axis badges

Separate Low / Med / High pills for Rude, Pattern, Scam/Bait, and Fake—each with its own color and explanation.

◎

Explainable popovers

Click any badge for reasons, matched phrases, and profile context. Draggable, scrollable detail panels.

◐

Soft-hide & snooze

Dim or collapse risky comments locally. Snooze authors for 24h, 7d, or forever—Facebook content is never removed.

✎

Custom phrases

Block and allow lists plus optional regex. Import/export JSON to move rules between machines.

▣

Image & meme comments

Uses alt text by default; optional on-device OCR (Tesseract) for text inside images—no cloud processing.

⚡

Thread heat & focus guard

See when subthreads flare up. Optional nudge to enable calmer dim mode after many high-risk comments.

How it works

Three steps. No account. No upload.

Install once, browse facebook.com on desktop, and let ThreadSense annotate what you already see.

Install & enable

Add ThreadSense from the Chrome Web Store (or load unpacked for testing). Toggle the extension on and open Facebook in a normal tab.

Scroll your feed

As comments appear, ThreadSense scores visible text and optional public profile cues. Badges attach next to comments and some posts.

Investigate & tune

Open popovers for context, adjust sensitivity, soft-hide, and custom phrases in Settings. You choose what to trust.

Scoring

Four lenses on risky conversation

Scores are 0–100 heuristics with Low / Medium / High bands. They can disagree—that is intentional.

Rude tone

Insults, shouting, condescension, and heated language detected from phrase lists and text signals.

Troll pattern

Repetition, multi-target hostility, copy-paste replies, and drive-by engagement patterns.

Scam / bait

Promotional scams, debate bait, coordinated talking points, and bait-style wording bundles.

Fake-account risk

Link spam, thin profiles, default avatars, burst activity—behavior cues, not identity verdicts.

Privacy-first

Your feed never leaves your browser

ThreadSense is designed for people who want signal without surveillance.

  • No analytics, ads, or remote logging
  • No selling or profiling of comment text
  • Optional OCR runs locally via Tesseract workers
  • Bug reports stay on-device until you choose to share them

FAQ

Common questions

Does ThreadSense report or block people for me?
No. It only changes how comments look on your screen (badges, dim, collapse) and never contacts Facebook or other users on your behalf.
Can it prove someone is a troll or fake account?
No. Scores are pattern-based guesses and can be wrong—especially with sarcasm, quotes, or jokes. Use them as one input among many.
What data leaves my computer?
By default, none of your feed content is sent to ThreadSense servers. Optional phrase-list updates fetch only public word lists. See the privacy policy for OCR and future license checks.
Does it work on mobile Facebook?
ThreadSense targets Facebook on desktop Chrome. Mobile browsers do not support this extension model.
Is ThreadSense affiliated with Meta?
No. ThreadSense is an independent project and is not endorsed by Meta Platforms, Inc.
